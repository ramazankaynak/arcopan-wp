## What to Include in Your Inquiry

For fastest response, provide:

- **Facility Details:** Size (m²), current state (new/existing), location
- **Project Type:** New build / Expansion / Replacement / Feasibility study
- **Temperature Requirements:** Target temperature range and tolerance (e.g., 0/+4°C ±1°C)
- **Product Type:** What will be stored (food, pharmaceutical, industrial, etc.)
- **Compliance Needs:** HACCP / GDP / GMP / ATEX / ISO / Other
- **Timeline:** When do you need design complete? Construction start?
- **Budget Range:** Rough budget or budget class (€100k–500k, €500k–1M, €1M+)
- **Contact:** Name, email, phone, company

The more detail you provide, the faster we can respond with preliminary scope and budget.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you offer on-site consultations?**
A: Yes. We can arrange on-site feasibility visits, facility walkthroughs, and design consultations. Typically scheduled within 1–2 weeks of initial contact.

**Q: Is there a cost for preliminary consultation?**
A: No. Initial consultations and feasibility assessment are complimentary. We only charge fees for detailed engineering and design work that leads to a formal proposal.

**Q: What's your typical response to a cold storage RFQ?**
A: Standard response timeline:

- Final quotation: 3–4 weeks

**Q: Can you support emergency repairs or urgent projects?**
A: Yes. We maintain 24/7 emergency support for active projects. Contact our emergency line and we'll dispatch technical support or engineering team as needed.

**Q: Do you provide on-site installation supervision?**
A: Yes. We can provide project management, installation supervision, commissioning, and operator training. Services scale from part-time oversight to full-time project management.

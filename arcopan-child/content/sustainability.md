## Intro
Sustainable cold chain infrastructure is not a future goal — it is an engineering requirement today. ARCOPAN integrates energy efficiency, responsible material sourcing and low-impact refrigerant selection into every project from the design stage.

## Our Commitments
- Energy Efficiency — All cooling systems specified to minimise energy consumption. Thermal performance calculations provided for every insulation scope.
- Low-GWP Refrigerants — We specify natural refrigerants and low-GWP alternatives wherever technically viable and compliant with F-Gas regulations.
- Material Responsibility — PIR/PUR insulation panels selected for long service life and recyclability. Minimised waste in panel fabrication.
- Project Longevity — Systems designed to last 20+ years, reducing total lifecycle carbon impact versus short-term solutions.

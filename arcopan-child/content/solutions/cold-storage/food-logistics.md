## Introduction

Food logistics cold stores operate as the critical hub of modern food supply chains — multi-temperature warehouses that receive, sort, store, and distribute perishable products across regions and continents. These facilities must maintain simultaneous chilled (+2 to +5°C), frozen (-18 to -25°C), and ambient zones, with precise logistics flows and compliance with complex food safety regulations.

ARCOPAN designs end-to-end food logistics facilities that integrate refrigeration, insulation, warehouse automation, and supply chain logistics into a single coordinated system.

## The Challenge

Food logistics warehouses represent the most complex cold storage application:

- **Multi-Temperature Zones:** Simultaneous chilled, frozen, and ambient areas within one facility
- **High Throughput:** 500–2,000+ daily shipments in/out require rapid dock cycles and logistics coordination
- **Food Safety Compliance:** HACCP, GDPR traceability, and temperature logging across entire supply chain
- **Energy Intensity:** Large facilities operate 24/7/365; energy costs can exceed €50k/month
- **Capital Intensity:** Modern food logistics facilities cost €5–15M and require 2–5 year amortization
- **Integration Complexity:** Refrigeration, materials handling, automation, and logistics systems must work in concert

## The ARCOPAN Approach

Food logistics systems require integrated end-to-end design:

### Facility Layout & Zoning
We design multi-zone facilities with separate chilled, frozen, and preparation areas. Loading dock design minimizes infiltration. Racking systems maximize cube utilization while maintaining product accessibility.

### Refrigeration System Design
Multiple independent refrigeration circuits maintain simultaneous temperature zones. Load analysis accounts for peak receiving, product mix, and dock door cycles.

### Automation & Tracking
Temperature logging, inventory management, and shipment tracking systems provide real-time visibility and compliance documentation.

### Supply Chain Integration
Full coordination with your existing supply chain systems — WMS integration, EDI compliance, and last-mile delivery coordination.

## Key Benefits

- **Compliance Certainty:** Full traceability and temperature logging for HACCP and regulatory compliance
- **Energy Efficiency:** Optimized layout and refrigeration design reduces operating costs by 20–30%
- **Throughput Optimization:** Dock design and racking maximize product flow and minimize dwell time
- **Supply Chain Integration:** Seamless coordination with ERP, WMS, and last-mile systems
- **Scalability:** Facilities scale from 500m² micro-fulfillment to 10,000m²+ regional distribution hubs

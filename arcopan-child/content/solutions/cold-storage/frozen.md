## Introduction

Frozen storage operates at the coldest extreme of food preservation — maintaining consistent temperatures between -18 and -25°C across facilities that demand absolute reliability. Frozen storage protects high-value products (meat, seafood, prepared foods) and supports critical supply chains across food service, distribution, and retail.

ARCOPAN's frozen storage systems are engineered to deliver precise temperature control, minimal product loss, and operational efficiency at the coldest end of the temperature spectrum.

## The Challenge

Frozen storage represents the most demanding cold storage application:

- **Deep Cold Requirements:** Maintaining -18 to -25°C requires robust refrigeration and superior insulation
- **Thermal Load Management:** Heavy defrost cycles and product rotation create variable cooling demands
- **Equipment Durability:** Compressors and refrigerant systems work at maximum capacity
- **Safety & Compliance:** Employee safety in ultra-cold environments; food safety certification mandatory
- **High Capital Cost:** Facilities are typically large-scale investments requiring long service life

## The ARCOPAN Approach

Frozen storage systems demand precision engineering:

### Refrigeration Sizing
We calculate defrost load, product load, and infiltration load to specify industrial-grade condensing units and evaporators rated for continuous -25°C operation. No oversizing — exact capacity matched to facility requirements.

### Insulation Excellence
PIR-core panels with U-values <0.15 W/m²K minimize heat transfer through the envelope. Superior insulation reduces refrigeration runtime and energy consumption by 20–30% vs. standard construction.

### Temperature Monitoring
Multi-zone temperature sensors, backup alarms, and automatic defrost scheduling prevent product loss from temperature excursions.

## Key Benefits

- **Deep Cold Efficiency:** Optimized refrigeration sizing reduces energy consumption at -25°C
- **Defrost Optimization:** Automatic defrost scheduling prevents ice buildup and maintains air circulation
- **Superior Insulation:** <0.15 W/m²K U-values minimize envelope heat loss
- **Safety Features:** Emergency backup systems, low-temperature alarms, personnel safety zones
- **Scalability:** Systems built to 5,000m²+ for major distribution facilities

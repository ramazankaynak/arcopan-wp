## Introduction

Blast freezing (shock freezing) is a specialized cold storage application that rapidly reduces product temperature from ambient or chilled to -25°C in hours rather than days. This rapid freeze process preserves product quality, texture, and nutritional value while enabling efficient supply chain logistics.

ARCOPAN's blast freezing systems are engineered for meat processing facilities, food manufacturers, and quick-freeze logistics operations where speed-to-frozen is a competitive advantage.

## The Technology

Blast freezing operates on a fundamentally different principle than standard cold storage:

- **High Air Velocity:** Powerful evaporator fans create 3–5 m/s air circulation to rapidly reduce surface temperature
- **Rapid Freeze Cycles:** Products transition from ambient to -25°C in 4–24 hours (vs. 48–72 hours in standard storage)
- **Quality Preservation:** Rapid freezing creates smaller ice crystals, minimizing cell membrane damage and product quality loss
- **Batch Operations:** Chambers cycle load-freeze-unload repeatedly, not continuous like standard cold storage

## The Challenge

Blast freezing demands specialized engineering:

- **High Refrigeration Load:** Rapid temperature reduction requires 2–3x the refrigeration capacity of static cold storage
- **Evaporator Design:** Must deliver extreme cold (-35°C supply air) to achieve -25°C product temperature
- **Humidity Control:** High air velocity creates moisture loss; systems must balance freeze speed with product shrinkage
- **Operational Complexity:** Defrost cycles, fan scheduling, and temperature profiling require precise controls
- **Food Quality:** Improper freezing damages texture and commercial value of meat/seafood

## The ARCOPAN Approach

Blast freezing systems demand specialized refrigeration engineering:

### Refrigeration Specification
We size primary refrigeration for continuous heavy-duty operation plus secondary low-temperature circuit for blast chamber supply. Cascade refrigeration systems enable -35°C supply air for rapid product freeze.

### Chamber Design
Insulated blast chambers with industrial fans optimized for high-velocity, low-moisture-loss air distribution. Temperature profiling ensures even freeze across product pallets.

### Automation & Control
Programmable logic controllers manage freeze cycles, defrost timing, and door interlocks. Real-time temperature monitoring prevents product quality loss.

## Key Benefits

- **Speed-to-Market:** 4–24 hour freeze cycles enable rapid supply chain logistics
- **Quality Preservation:** Rapid freezing vs. slow freeze improves product texture and shelf life by 30–40%
- **High Throughput:** Batch operation cycles 100–200 tonnes per day through a single facility
- **Compliance Ready:** Designed for HACCP and food safety standards
- **Operational Control:** Automated cycles reduce labour and human error

## Applications

Blast freezing is standard in:

- **Meat Processing:** Carcass, cut, and ground meat rapid freeze
- **Seafood Processing:** Fish, shrimp, and prepared seafood
- **Food Manufacturing:** Prepared meals, sauces, and specialty foods
- **Bakery & Pastry:** Dough, pastries, and prepared baked goods

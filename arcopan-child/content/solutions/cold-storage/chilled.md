## Introduction

Chilled storage is the foundation of modern food supply chains. From fresh produce distribution to dairy product storage, maintaining precise temperature control between 0 and +4°C is critical for product quality, safety and shelf life. ARCOPAN designs and delivers complete chilled storage systems engineered for your exact operational requirements.

Our approach combines high-performance insulation, precise refrigeration control, and industrial-grade construction to create reliable chilled facilities across food, beverage, and pharmaceutical sectors.

## The Challenge

Chilled storage presents unique engineering demands:

- **Temperature Precision:** ±1°C accuracy required across large chambers
- **Energy Efficiency:** Continuous cooling operations drive utility costs
- **Food Safety Compliance:** HACCP and regulatory requirements must be met
- **Operational Continuity:** Downtime equals product loss and safety risk
- **Scale Variability:** From 50m² fresh-cut facilities to 2,000m²+ distribution centres

## The ARCOPAN Approach

We solve chilled storage through integrated system design:

### System Design Phase
Full thermal load calculation, refrigeration cycle selection, and layout optimization. We account for product load, ambient conditions, traffic patterns, and future expansion requirements.

### Component Selection
Precise refrigeration sizing — we specify condensing units, evaporators, and controls matched to your facility's thermal profile. PIR/PUR-core insulated panels minimize heat transfer and energy waste.

### Installation & Commissioning
Full supervision of installation, refrigerant charging, temperature mapping, and operator training. We verify system performance before handover.

## Key Benefits

- **Energy Efficiency:** Optimized insulation and refrigeration sizing reduce operational costs by 15–25% vs. oversized systems
- **Temperature Control:** ±0.5°C accuracy with precision thermostats and backup alarms
- **Compliance Ready:** Pre-designed for HACCP, GHP, and food safety standards
- **Scalable Design:** Systems expand from 50m² to 2,000m²+ without redesign
- **Rapid Deployment:** Modular panel construction enables 30–60 day build cycles

## Product Introduction
Industrial door systems for logistics, cold store and manufacturing facilities. High-speed roller doors, sectional overhead doors and insulated cold store access doors — all specified for energy efficiency, traffic frequency and temperature control requirements.

## Key Specifications
- High-speed doors: open speed up to 2.5 m/s, cycle life 500,000+
- Sectional doors: panel thickness 40–80mm, U-value from 1.0 W/m²K
- Width: up to 6m / Height: up to 6m standard (custom available)
- Drive: Electric motor with fail-safe manual override
- Safety: Photo-electric safety edge, bottom edge protection

## Product Introduction
Market racking systems for retail, supermarket and wholesale environments — designed for fresh produce, dairy and chilled product display. Hygienic, corrosion-resistant and configurable to store layout.

## Key Specifications
- Material: Galvanised steel or stainless steel
- Shelf load: up to 250 kg per shelf
- Adjustable shelf pitch: 50mm increments
- Finish: Powder coat or hot-dip galvanised
- Temperature range: 0°C to +15°C
- Hygienic: Easy-clean surfaces, open-frame design

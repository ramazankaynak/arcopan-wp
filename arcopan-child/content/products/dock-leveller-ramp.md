## Product Introduction
Hydraulic and mechanical dock levellers and loading ramps for cold chain warehouses and distribution centres. Designed for high-frequency use, compatibility with all standard truck bed heights and safe operation in low-temperature loading bay environments.

## Key Specifications
- Type: Hydraulic / Mechanical / Vertical storing
- Capacity: 6,000 kg standard (up to 9,000 kg heavy duty)
- Platform size: 2,000 x 2,500mm standard (custom available)
- Lip length: 400mm / 500mm
- Temperature rating: -20°C to +40°C
- Safety: Non-slip surface, safety strut, toe guard

## Product Introduction
Industrial condensing units for cold room and freezer store applications. Air-cooled and water-cooled variants available across a wide capacity range — suitable for standalone cold rooms through to multi-evaporator systems.

## Key Specifications
- Type: Air-cooled / Water-cooled
- Capacity: 2 kW to 150 kW
- Refrigerants: R404A, R448A, R449A, R134a, R290
- Compressor: Hermetic, semi-hermetic, scroll
- Ambient temperature range: -20°C to +45°C
- Protection: IP54 minimum, IP65 available

## Product Introduction
ARCOPAN insulated cold storage panels use a PIR or PUR foam core bonded between profiled steel facings. Engineered for cold rooms, blast freezers and industrial refrigeration facilities requiring high thermal performance, structural integrity and long service life.

## Key Specifications
- Core: PIR (polyisocyanurate) or PUR (polyurethane)
- Thickness: 80 / 100 / 120 / 150 / 200mm
- U-value: from 0.18 W/m²K (150mm PIR)
- Facing: Profiled steel, stainless steel or food-grade liner options
- Temperature range: -35°C to +15°C
- Fire classification: Euroclass B, C or D depending on specification
- Certification: EN 13165, CE Marking

## Applications
- Cold rooms and freezer stores
- Blast freezing chambers
- Pharmaceutical temperature-controlled rooms
- Food production clean rooms
- Logistics cold store facilities

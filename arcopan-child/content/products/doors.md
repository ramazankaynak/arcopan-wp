## Product Introduction
Cold room doors are a critical thermal and safety element of any cold storage facility. ARCOPAN supplies insulated walk-in doors, sliding cold room doors and high-traffic industrial variants — each engineered for airtight sealing, safety compliance and operational durability.

## Key Specifications
- Insulation core: PIR, 80–120mm
- Temperature range: -35°C to +15°C
- Sealing: Magnetic gasket, heated frame option for sub-zero
- Safety: Interior release mechanism standard on all walk-in doors
- Finish: Stainless steel or food-grade white
- Options: Vision panel, electric operation, traffic protection frame

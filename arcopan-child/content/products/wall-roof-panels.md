## Product Introduction
Insulated wall and roof panel systems for large-span cold storage facilities and refrigerated warehouses. Designed for structural performance as well as thermal efficiency — suitable for new-build and refurbishment projects.

## Key Specifications
- Core: PIR foam, density 40–42 kg/m³
- Panel width: 1000mm / 1200mm standard
- Length: up to 12m (custom available)
- Roof load capacity: up to 1.5 kN/m²
- Joint system: tongue-and-groove with cam-lock fixings
- Facing options: Galvanised steel, stainless, food-grade white

## Applications
- Refrigerated warehouses
- Cold store distribution centres
- Food logistics facilities
- Large-format blast freeze chambers

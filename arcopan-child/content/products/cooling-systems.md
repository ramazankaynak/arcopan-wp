## Product Introduction
Complete industrial cooling systems engineered for cold storage facilities from 50m² to 20,000m²+. ARCOPAN coordinates system design, equipment selection, installation and commissioning — delivering a single accountable cooling scope.

## Key Specifications
- Capacity range: 5 kW to 500 kW+
- Temperature range: -35°C to +15°C
- Refrigerants: R404A, R448A, R449A, CO₂ (R744), NH₃ (R717)
- Configuration: Single circuit, parallel rack, cascade systems
- Controls: Electronic expansion valve, remote monitoring capable
- Compliance: F-Gas regulation compliant, low-GWP options available

## Applications
- Chilled storage (0/+4°C)
- Frozen storage (-18/-25°C)
- Blast freezing (-35°C)
- Process cooling and controlled atmosphere

## Product Introduction
Heavy-duty pallet racking and drive-in racking systems engineered for cold store and freezer warehouse environments. All systems are rated for low-temperature operation and comply with EN 15512 structural standards.

## Key Specifications
- Type: Selective pallet racking / Drive-in / Push-back / Narrow aisle
- Beam load: up to 4,000 kg per level
- Frame height: up to 12m
- Temperature rating: -30°C to +30°C
- Finish: Epoxy powder coat, low-temperature rated
- Standard: EN 15512 / FEM 10.2.02

## Product Introduction
Industrial evaporator units for cold storage and process cooling applications. Ceiling-mounted and wall-mounted configurations, with electric, hot gas and off-cycle defrost options to suit application temperature and duty cycle.

## Key Specifications
- Type: Ceiling mount / Wall mount / Unit cooler
- Capacity: 1 kW to 80 kW
- Temperature range: -40°C to +15°C
- Defrost: Electric / Hot gas / Off-cycle
- Fin spacing: 4mm to 12mm (application dependent)
- Casing: Galvanised or stainless steel

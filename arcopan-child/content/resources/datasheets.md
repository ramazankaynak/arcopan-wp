## Specifications & Standards

All ARCOPAN products comply with applicable European standards:

- **EN 13165** — Thermal insulation products for building applications
- **EN 13166–13170** — Rigid foam insulation, polyurethane, polystyrene standards
- **EN 12079** — Insulated assemblies for industrial applications
- **ISO 9001** — Quality management certification (all ARCOPAN manufacturing)
- **CE Marking** — Product safety conformity declaration

## Quality & Compliance Certifications

ARCOPAN maintains comprehensive quality and compliance certifications across manufacturing, products, and services.

## Manufacturing Certifications

### ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management Systems

ARCOPAN manufacturing facilities are ISO 9001 certified, ensuring consistent product quality across all production lines. Certification covers:

- Design and development of insulated panel systems
- Manufacturing processes for PIR, PUR, and specialty cores
- Quality control testing and verification
- Document management and traceability

**Scope:** Insulated panel manufacturing, refrigeration system integration, final assembly and testing

**Audit Frequency:** Annual third-party audits by accredited certification bodies

## Product Certifications

### EN 13165 — Thermal Insulation Products

ARCOPAN insulated panels comply with EN 13165 specifications for thermal insulation products for building applications:

- **Thermal Performance:** U-value specifications verified through independent testing
- **Reaction to Fire:** Material safety ratings for fire exposure
- **Dimensional Stability:** Panel dimensional tolerance verification
- **Durability:** Long-term performance testing across environmental conditions

→ [Download EN 13165 Test Report](/contact)

### EN 13166–13170 — Foam Insulation Standards

All ARCOPAN core materials (PIR, PUR, polystyrene) comply with applicable EN standards:

- **EN 13166** — Rigid polyurethane foam (PUR)
- **EN 13167** — Rigid polyisocyanurate foam (PIR)
- **EN 13170** — Extruded polystyrene foam (XPS)

Each core material is independently tested and certified by accredited laboratories.

→ [Download Foam Material Certificates](/contact)

### EN 12079 — Insulated Assemblies

ARCOPAN insulated panel assemblies are tested and verified under EN 12079 specifications for complete systems:

- **Thermal Performance:** Full assembly U-value verification (not component-level)
- **Air Permeability:** Envelope sealing and infiltration testing
- **Moisture Performance:** Vapor barrier effectiveness and moisture management
- **Mechanical Performance:** Load-bearing capacity and durability

→ [Download EN 12079 Test Report](/contact)

### CE Marking — Product Safety Conformity

All ARCOPAN products carry CE marking, indicating compliance with applicable European product directives:

- **Building Products Regulation (CPR):** Construction product safety and performance
- **Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC:** Safety for equipment and systems
- **Low Voltage Directive 2014/35/EU:** Electrical equipment safety

→ [Download CE Conformity Declaration](/contact)

## Refrigeration & Safety Certifications

### PED — Pressure Equipment Directive (2014/68/EU)

ARCOPAN refrigeration systems comply with PED requirements for pressure equipment safety:

- **System Design:** Pressure vessel and piping engineering per PED standards
- **Material Selection:** Refrigerant compatibility and safety ratings
- **Certification:** Third-party design review and approval

→ [Download PED Certification](/contact)

### ATEX — Explosive Atmosphere Equipment (2014/34/EU)

ARCOPAN can design and deliver ATEX-certified cold storage systems for hazardous atmosphere environments (e.g., grain stores, solvent storage):

- **Electrical Equipment:** Non-sparking, temperature-limited components
- **System Design:** Hazard analysis and risk mitigation
- **Installation:** Specialized procedures for explosive environments

→ [Request ATEX Certification Details](/contact)

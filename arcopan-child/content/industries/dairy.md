## Dairy: Temperature Precision Engineering

The dairy industry requires absolute temperature precision. From raw milk reception (requiring near-freezing temperatures) to cheese aging (requiring specific humidity and temperature zones) to finished product storage, dairy facilities operate within tight temperature windows critical for product quality and food safety.

ARCOPAN has engineered dairy storage facilities with precision temperature control and humidity management across Europe and the Middle East.

## Industry Challenges

Dairy producers face specialized cold storage requirements:

- **Extreme Temperature Precision:** Raw milk storage at 2–4°C requires ±1°C accuracy across receiving tanks
- **Multi-Zone Complexity:** Receiving, aging, processing, and finished goods stored at different temperatures
- **Humidity Control:** Cheese aging and specialty products require specific humidity ranges in addition to temperature
- **Long-Duration Storage:** Some dairy products are stored for months or years, requiring perfect consistency
- **Regulatory Compliance:** HACCP, pasteurization standards, and traceability requirements
- **Energy Economics:** Continuous refrigeration is a significant cost driver in dairy production

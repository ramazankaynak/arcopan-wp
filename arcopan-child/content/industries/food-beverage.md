## Food & Beverage: The Cold Chain Foundation

The food and beverage industry depends on reliable cold storage at every step: production, processing, storage, and distribution. From farm to retail, temperature control is non-negotiable for food safety, quality, regulatory compliance, and supply chain economics.

ARCOPAN has engineered cold storage solutions for food and beverage manufacturers, processors, and distributors across Europe and the Middle East for 30+ years.

## Industry Challenges

Food and beverage producers face unique cold storage requirements:

- **Temperature Precision:** Maintaining exact specifications for fresh produce, dairy, and prepared foods
- **Rapid Throughput:** High-volume receiving and shipping demand rapid dock cycles and logistics coordination
- **Food Safety Compliance:** HACCP certification, EU food safety regulations, and traceability requirements
- **Energy Costs:** Continuous refrigeration operations are a significant operating expense
- **Product Quality:** Temperature fluctuations damage freshness, shelf life, and commercial value

## Meat & Poultry: Precision Cold Engineering

Meat and poultry processing demands the most specialized cold storage engineering. From carcass storage to cut processing to rapid blast freezing to finished product distribution, every stage requires exact temperature control and rapid logistics.

ARCOPAN specializes in meat and poultry cold storage facilities with blast freezing capability, thermographic traceability, and HACCP compliance across the processing workflow.

## Industry Challenges

Meat and poultry producers face complex cold storage requirements:

- **Multiple Temperature Zones:** Carcass holding, cut processing, blast freezing, and frozen storage in one facility
- **Rapid Freeze Capability:** Blast freezing (shock freeze) is essential for quality preservation and product shelf life
- **Food Safety Compliance:** HACCP certification is mandatory; temperature traceability is critical
- **Throughput Optimization:** High-volume daily processing requires precise logistics and dock design
- **Product Quality:** Temperature fluctuations damage meat quality and reduce shelf life by days
- **Regulatory Documentation:** EU food safety regulations require complete temperature logging and audit trails

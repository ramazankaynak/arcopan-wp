## Pharmaceuticals: Regulatory Certainty Engineering

Pharmaceutical cold storage operates under the most stringent regulatory requirements. Good Distribution Practice (GDP) compliance is mandatory; GMP standards govern facility design; temperature precision and complete audit trails are non-negotiable.

ARCOPAN has engineered GDP-compliant pharmaceutical cold storage facilities for major pharma distributors and manufacturers across Europe and the Middle East.

## Industry Challenges

Pharmaceutical cold storage presents the most demanding regulatory environment:

- **GDP Compliance (Medicinal Products Directive):** Temperature ranges typically 2–8°C with ±1°C tolerance; excursions must be documented
- **GMP Standards:** Manufacturing and distribution facilities must meet Good Manufacturing Practice standards
- **Audit Trail Requirements:** Every temperature reading, every door opening, every access must be logged and auditable
- **Stability Testing:** Temperature data is critical for product stability claims and regulatory approvals
- **Batch Integrity:** Medications stored in lot-controlled compartments; batch segregation is mandatory
- **Continuous Monitoring:** 24/7/365 operation with immediate alarm notification for any excursion
- **Third-Party Compliance:** Distributors must pass regulatory inspections from national medicines agencies

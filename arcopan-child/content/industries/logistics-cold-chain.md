## Cold Chain Logistics: Supply Chain Engineering

Cold chain logistics is the critical infrastructure enabling global food trade. Modern supply chains depend on interconnected logistics hubs that maintain perfect temperature control while moving thousands of shipments daily across regions and continents.

ARCOPAN has engineered mega-scale cold chain logistics facilities for major distributors, 3PLs, and logistics operators across Europe and EMEA.

## Industry Challenges

Cold chain logistics operators face extreme complexity:

- **Multi-Temperature Zones:** Simultaneous chilled, frozen, and ambient zones within single facility
- **Extreme Throughput:** 500–2,000+ daily shipments in/out require optimization of dock cycles and logistics flows
- **Temperature Compliance:** Every shipment must arrive at destination within temperature range; excursions create liability
- **Traceability Requirement:** Full chain-of-custody and temperature logging for every shipment
- **Energy Intensity:** 24/7/365 operation with multiple independent refrigeration zones drives substantial costs
- **Capital Intensity:** Facilities cost €5–15M+; long amortization period requires high utilization and efficiency
- **Integration Complexity:** WMS, TMS, and logistics systems must coordinate with refrigeration and dock operations
